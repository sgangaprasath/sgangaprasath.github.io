Program

This 4 day workshop with hands-on demo & mini-projects is aimed at late undergraduate and early Masters students interested in behavioural science, computer vision, physical simulation and modeling.

Certificates will be provided for participants who successfully complete the mini-projects*.

* - Mini-projects need to be submitted on 17 January 2025

Format

2 lectures, 2 hand-on demo, Total 6 hours & mini-project

Topics

  • Computational Ethology

    1. Ethograms
    2. Classification techniques
    3. Markerless tracking

  • Agent-based and Continuum models

    1. Couzin & Social-force models
    2. Fokker-Planck dynamics
    3. ML based Equation discovery
