S Ganga Prasath

Asst. Prof. at IIT Madras

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Applied Mechanics at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras. Our group is interested in problems at the interface of robotics, behaviour and soft-matter. You can check out the INTERFACE lab page for more details.

Outside work, I have a set of projects that keep me busy. You can access their progress through the projects page.

Write to me if you are interested in collaborating or would like to know more about any of these topics.
